How It Works

Our proprietary instrumentation suite monitors and controls the entire carbon lifecycle—from
combustion exhaust to underground storage:

Flue Gas Analytics

Advanced NDIR and TDLAS analyzers measure CO₂, CH₄, and NOₓ emissions with precision.

Flow & Capture Optimization

Intelligent flow meters and capture inlet sensors deliver actionable data to fine-tune efficiency.

Capture Verification

Post-capture analyzers validate CO₂ purity and throughput, ensuring your carbon credits are real—and redeemable.

Storage Traceability

Integrated sensors track injection volumes and detect leaks at storage sites, supporting full MRV compliance.

All data is logged via secure SCADA/PLC systems and packaged for regulatory and blockchain-based carbon credit programs.
